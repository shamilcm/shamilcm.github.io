I am Research Scientist at Rakuten Institute of Technology (Rakuten, Inc.). I completed my PhD in natural language processing (NLP) at the National University of Singapore (NUS) under the supervision of Prof. Ng Hwee Tou. During my PhD, I was supported by the NGS Scholarship. Before joining NUS, I completed my Bachelor's in Computer Science and Engineering from the National Institute of Technology Calicut, India (old website).

My research interests include natural language processing and deep learning
