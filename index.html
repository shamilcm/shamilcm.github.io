I am a PhD candidate working in natural language processing at the National University of Singapore (NUS) supported by the NGS Scholarship. Before joining NUS, I completed my Bachelor's in Computer Science and Engineering from the National Institute of Technology Calicut, India. See what I did back then in my older website.

My current research is focussed on applying deep learning to grammatical error correction, information extraction, and question answering.
